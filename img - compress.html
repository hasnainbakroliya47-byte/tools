Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about image compression

How does image compression work?

Image compression reduces file size by removing unnecessary metadata and optimizing image data. Our tool uses smart algorithms to maintain visual quality while significantly reducing file size.

Will compression reduce image quality?

With proper settings, you can achieve significant file size reduction with minimal quality loss. Our balanced preset provides the best compromise between size and quality.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can convert between these formats or keep the original format. WebP offers the best compression but may not be supported by all browsers.

Are my images safe?

Yes! All processing happens locally in your browser. We never upload your images to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security. Your images stay on your device.
